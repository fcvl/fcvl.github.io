Explanantion of the picture

We input 6 variables as a set of parameters, and the two-dimensional cloud map drawn is the hydrogen distribution in the flow field. The flow field is 50cm long and 6cm wide. As shown in the figure above, the horizontal axis is the long side and the vertical axis is the wide side. This flow channel is parallel, The left end is the entrance, so the concentration of hydrogen is higher, and the closer to the exit, the lower the concentration of hydrogen.
